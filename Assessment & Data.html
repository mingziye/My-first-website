· Assessment & Data ·

The kinds of data that would be needed to assess it include:

* Superkilen App – Location information app about each object and position in the park.

* Placing sensors in recycle bins to monitor waste and notify waste removal management teams on precise scheduled collection times.

* Temperature sensors – with LED display screens at locations on site to notify visitors about daily temperatures.

* Motion sensors – to collect data about the number of visitors to the park. Also integrated into park lights at night to collect

visitors to the site at night.
